Fossil Funds LLC

Fossil Fundsis a private investment group based in the Woodlands, TX, founded by four partners with a shared background in energy. We started Fossil Funds as an opportunity to work with good people and help diversity ourselves. We invest in small businesses and cash-flowing real estate, focusing on operational improvement and long-term value creation. We take pride in our work and the communities we live and raise our families in. An ideal company would not only be an enduringly profitable but one that is positioned to give back to the community that it does business in.

Investment Criteria

  • Businesses located in Houston or Oklahoma City
  • Small Business and Real Estate opportunities between $500,000-$3,000,000
  • Passive preferred, can provide business operator
  • Enduring Profitable Businesses
  • Preferred industries: Liquor Store, Franchise, Self-Storage, Laundromats, Real Estate
