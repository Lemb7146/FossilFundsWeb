Fossil Funds LLC

Fossil Funds is a team of experienced energy professionals focused on acquiring and operating small businesses with long-term growth potential. We bring a disciplined approach to business ownership, combining technical expertise with operational excellence.

Investment Criteria

  • Profitable U.S.-based business
  • $500K – $5M in annual revenue
  • Stable cash flow and operational history
  • Owners looking to transition or exit
  • Service-based or light industrial sectors preferred
